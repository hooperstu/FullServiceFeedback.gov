Your feedback matters

Help us improve our bereavement services at HMRC. By sharing your experience, you’ll help us understand what we’re doing well and where we can improve, so we can better support others during difficult times.

Who can provide feedback

This service is open to anyone who has interacted with HMRC’s bereavement services. This includes:

  • Family members or friends of the deceased
  • Executors or administrators of the estate
  • Legal or financial representatives of the bereaved

What to expect

This survey should take around 5 minutes to complete. Your feedback is anonymous, and your responses will only be used to improve HMRC’s services.
